
Description: Computes the real-time equivalent of an accelerated ageing study using
             the Q10 method (ASTM F1980). Reports the acceleration factor and real-time
             equivalent duration, with sensitivity to Q10 +/- 0.5.

Usage: jrc_shelf_life_q10 <q10> <accel_temp> <real_temp> <accel_time>

Arguments:
	q10           Q10 coefficient — factor by which reaction rate increases per
	              10 degrees C rise in temperature. ASTM F1980 default is 2.0.
	              Typical range: 1.5-3.0. Use experimental data to justify
	              values outside this range.
	accel_temp    Accelerated storage temperature (degrees C).
	real_temp     Real-time storage temperature (degrees C). Must be lower than
	              accel_temp.
	accel_time    Duration of accelerated ageing in any consistent time unit
	              (days, weeks, months). The real-time result will be in the
	              same unit.
	-h|--help     Show this help message.

Formula:
	AF         = Q10 ^ ((T_accel - T_real) / 10)
	real_time  = accel_time x AF

Output:
	Acceleration factor, real-time equivalent, and a sensitivity table
	showing real-time equivalents at Q10 - 0.5, Q10, and Q10 + 0.5.

Notes:
	ASTM F1980 requires that real-time ageing studies are conducted in
	parallel to confirm accelerated ageing claims before shelf life labelling.

	The Q10 method is appropriate for sterile barrier and packaging
	integrity. Device functionality, biocompatibility, and material
	stability may not follow Q10 kinetics and require separate assessment.

	For biologics, enzymes, or drug-device combinations, use Arrhenius
	kinetics with an experimentally derived activation energy
	(jrc_shelf_life_arrhenius).

Example:
	jrc_shelf_life_q10 2.0 55 25 26

	Computes the real-time equivalent of 26 weeks at 55 degrees C
	compared to 25 degrees C real-time storage, using Q10 = 2.0.
	Result: AF = 8.0, real-time equivalent = 208 weeks.

Reference:
	ASTM F1980-21, Standard Guide for Accelerated Aging of Sterile
	Barrier Systems for Medical Devices, ASTM International.
