Description: Provides Gauge R&R study design guidance based on AIAG MSA (Measurement
             Systems Analysis) rules. Given a target %GRR and reference value, reports
             the number of distinct categories (ndc) the measurement system can resolve,
             an AIAG acceptance verdict, and a table of operator/replicate combinations
             showing total measurements and degrees of freedom.

Usage: jrc_msa_grr_design "grr" "type" "sigma_or_tolerance"

Arguments:
       grr                  target %GRR as a percentage (e.g. 10 for 10%, 30 for 30%).
                            Must be between 0 and 100 (exclusive).
       type                 how %GRR is expressed:
                              "process"   — %GRR as a percentage of process variation.
                                            sigma_or_tolerance is the estimated process
                                            standard deviation (sigma).
                              "tolerance" — %GRR as a percentage of the tolerance
                                            (USL - LSL). sigma_or_tolerance is the
                                            tolerance width.
       sigma_or_tolerance   if type="process":   estimated process standard deviation
                            if type="tolerance": tolerance width (USL - LSL).
                            Must be a positive number.
       -h| --help           Show this help message

Output:
       Reports sigma_total, sigma_gauge, sigma_parts, and ndc.
       ndc (number of distinct categories) is the key acceptance metric:
         ndc >= 5   acceptable measurement system
         ndc >= 2   marginal
         ndc <  2   inadequate

       %GRR thresholds (AIAG MSA, 4th edition):
         %GRR < 10%   excellent
         %GRR < 30%   may be acceptable depending on application
         %GRR >= 30%  measurement system needs improvement

       The AIAG baseline study design (10 parts x 3 operators x 2 replicates) is
       highlighted in the study design table.

Example:
       jrc_msa_grr_design "10" "process" "0.5"
       jrc_msa_grr_design "10" "tolerance" "5.0"
